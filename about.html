About the restaurant

This restaurant started in the year 2013 in Virginia west America by DR. Andrew Greene when he was 22 years of age.

He started with a little tako shop where people would come and eat some meals with some drinks.

Later on the business started to fall and he was wise enough to start looking for investors of which it didn't turn out as. But luckily he got one investor who helped him to restore his bussiness.

After a couple of years he started opening branches all over the States and eventually after eight years of hard work the branches reached Africa and in East Africa Kenya was the first country the restaurant reached.
